Rummy Marroki Data Deletion Instructions

We respect your privacy. If you would like to delete all personal data associated with your Rummy Marroki account, you can do so by following the steps below — either directly through Facebook or by contacting us via email.

Option A — Remove via Facebook Settings

1

Go to your Facebook Settings

Open Facebook and navigate to Settings & Privacy → Settings.

2

Open Apps and Websites

In the left sidebar, click Apps and Websites. You will see a list of all apps connected to your Facebook account.

3

Find Rummy Marroki

Locate Rummy Marroki in the list and click on it to open the app settings.

4

Remove the app

Click Remove. This will disconnect Rummy Marroki from your Facebook account and revoke all permissions we were granted.

— OR —

Option B — Request via Email

Send us an email with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include your Facebook name or User ID.

We will permanently delete all your data within 30 days and send you a confirmation.

✉ Send Deletion Request

⚠ What will be deleted: your account profile, virtual coin balance, game history, and all personal data linked to your Facebook login. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
